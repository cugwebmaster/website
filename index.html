We are 30 to 50 enthusiastic musicians on any given Tuesday night...

...and you are welcome to join us, preferably with a ukulele , though other instruments such as guitars, violins and harmonicas are also welcome.

Why

Learning the ukulele is fun and a beginner-friendly way to explore music. Joining a group like ours lets you practice in a supportive social setting while improving your skills and getting tips from both other beginners and more experienced players.

When

We meet every Tuesday night, 7pm - 9pm, with a break at 8pm for a drink and a chat.

Where

The Cashmere Club, 50 Colombo Street and 88 Hunter Terrace, usually in the Garden Bar, along the entry corridor and turn left.

Let's Jam!

When you arrive, register at the door ( $2 koha to cover costs), grab any free seat and introduce yourself to those around you. Alternately, arrive early for a meal - check out the Cashmere Club Menu.


We have a few "loaner" songbooks for visitors and you can purchase your own copy when you are ready.
Or you can access our Songs online while jamming, with a tablet or laptop and a data plan, or download the songbook to a tablet or laptop and bring it along. See the Library page for more details.


The music leader will commence with a couple of songs then call for requests, usually alphabetically by first name.
