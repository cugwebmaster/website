Welcome to Cashmere Ukulele Group

Everyone is welcome, preferably with a ukulele , though other instruments such as guitars, violins and harmonicas are also welcome

Join Us

We meet every Tuesday night, 7pm - 9pm, with a break at 8pm for a drink and a chat.

The Venue

The Cashmere Club, 50 Colombo Street and 88 Hunter Terrace, usually in the Garden Bar, along the entry corridor and turn left

Let's Jam!

When you arrive, register at the door ($2 koha to cover costs) and grab any free seat.
Alternately, arrive early for a meal, check out the menu - Cashmere Club Bar
The music leader will commence with a couple of songs then call for requests - usually alphabetically by first name. Our songbook is here - Songs
